9 - ST. TIMOTHY

We are 9 - St. Timothy from Marist School Marikina, and we have been assigned the meaningful task of helping the children at St. Maria De Matthias Center for Children. Our mission is to bring them joy, laughter, and unforgettable experiences through engaging activities. We strive to create a positive impact in their lives by fostering connections, spreading kindness, and making every moment special.
