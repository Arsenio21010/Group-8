Objectives

To bring joy and laughter

This goal is about spreading happiness and creating moments of fun, helping to brighten people's moods and foster a positive environment.

To help those in need

This objective focuses on offering support to those who are struggling, whether through acts of kindness, volunteering, or providing resources, making a difference in their lives.

To make friends and be a good example

This aim is about forming meaningful relationships based on kindness and trust, while also living in a way that inspires others to do the same, creating a sense of community.
